Git Hub Guide

Git Hub Guide step by step

  1. Go to www.github.com
  2. Navigate to the upper right hand corner and click sign up
  3. Enter your email address
  4. Create a secured password
  5. Enter a username
  6. Verify your account and then click create account
  7. Personalize your account, asked for number of team members and specify if you are a student or teacher
  8. Click free membership
  9. from the dashboard screen, you click create a repository
  10. Follow the directions to specify settings needed and click create repository
